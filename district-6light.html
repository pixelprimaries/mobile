DISTRICT 6

Illinois's 6th congressional district is a congressional district in the U.S. state of Illinois. The district covers parts of Cook and DuPage counties.

Economically, the 6th District is relatively affluent, with a median household income of $94,711. The region is predominantly urban, characterized by a mix of residential neighborhoods, commercial centers, and industrial areas.​ Politically, the district has experienced notable shifts over recent election cycles. Historically a Republican stronghold, it transitioned to Democratic representation in 2018 when Sean Casten, a scientist and clean energy entrepreneur, was elected as the U.S. Representative. Casten has focused his legislative efforts on combating climate change and promoting sustainable energy solutions. The 6th District is characterized by its engaged electorate and active civic participation. Residents prioritize issues such as healthcare, education, infrastructure, and environmental sustainability. The district's proximity to Chicago provides both challenges and opportunities, influencing its economic development and policy priorities.
